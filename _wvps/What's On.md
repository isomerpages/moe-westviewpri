<h3>Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Annoucements/Arif_Budiman_Malay_Language_Teacher_Award.png">
</div>
<p>The Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) is an annual award
which recognises outstanding Malay Language teachers who have made significant
contributions to the teaching and learning of Malay language in our schools.
We would like to invite you to nominate your Malay Language teachers now!</p>
<p>Nominations can be done via the following URL: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/agab2024</a>
</p>
<h3>Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Annoucements/Most_Inspo_Tamil_Teacher.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award (MITT) is here again! This award
seeks to recognise Tamil Language teachers who have made significant contributions
to the teaching and learning of Tamil language in schools.</p>
<p>We would like to invite you to nominate your Tamil Language teachers now!</p>
<p>Nominations can be done via the following URL: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/mitt2024nominationformel" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mitt2024nominationformel</a>
</p>
