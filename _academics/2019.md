Reading Superstar Competition 2019
----------------------------------

This year, our school has been working with the Chinese Language Unit (Primary) of MOE’s Curriculum Planning and Development Division and experimenting with new approaches to the Chinese language reading programme. Pupils shared their love for reading through designing an extravaganza of book covers, project displays and book reviews. Their work were sent in to participate in the annual Reading Superstar Competition.

  

We are pleased to share that one of our pupils Ker Zhi Yang (6 Empathy) has been awarded the Merit Award (Upper Primary Category). We hope with this recognition, the programme can continue to bring our pupils’ interest in reading to the next level, encouraging them to cultivate reading as a habit.

  

Congratulations to Zhi Yang for a job well done!
