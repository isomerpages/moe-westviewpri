<h2>8. Chinese New Year celebration</h2>
<div class="iframe-wrapper">
<iframe allowfullscreen="true" frameborder="0" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?height=314&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fwestviewpri%2Fvideos%2F1130672138125791%2F&amp;show_text=true&amp;width=560&amp;t=0"></iframe>

<p>Our pupils experienced a vibrant and engaging Chinese New Year Celebration
last week. They were captivated by the different performances and items,
including the mesmerizing dragon and lion dances, as well as the chinese
flag dance. On top of that, our Chinese Dance troupe delivered an energetic
dance item featuring a minority group's culture.</p>
<p>Pupils from different levels showcased their talents in wu shu (martial
arts), chinese allegro, modern street dance, and singing. All these added
to the festive atmosphere of the event.</p>
