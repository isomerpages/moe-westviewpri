Fire, a symbol of passion and determination, burned bright in West View Primary as we celebrated our nation’s birthday with fun and games. Guests and pupils were welcomed by vibrant dancing tubes in the 5 Olympic colours. After the observance ceremony and the reading of the National Day message, the day’s main highlight began with the lighting of the Olympic torch by Mr Foo, Mr Quek and Mdm Kum. As the air horn sounded, all pupils rushed to the various stations, manned by our very own teachers, to participate in an array of fun carnival games, all in hopes of clinching the gold medal at the end of the day.
