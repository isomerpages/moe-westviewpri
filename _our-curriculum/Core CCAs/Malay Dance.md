Brief on CCA
------------

The Malay Dance CCA provides a platform for pupils to learn about Malay Dance, Malay music and&nbsp;Malay culture. Pupils are given opportunities to perform for the community and during school&nbsp;celebration.&nbsp;

  

Learning together, pupils inculcate the values of perseverance and continue to strive for&nbsp;excellence.
