<h4>Aesthetics Week</h4>
<p>This is a week-long celebration featuring captivating art exhibits, melodious
tunes, and interactive events. During this event, pupils unleash their
creativity as they dive into a vibrant world of Art and Music and West
View Primary School gets transformed into a canvas of inspiration. At Aesthetics
week, passion for aesthetics stay ignited – a masterpiece in the making!</p>

<h4>Birthday Celebrations</h4>
<p>Every month, the whole school comes together tocelebrate each staff and
pupil's special day with present, singing, laughter, and unforgettable
moments. It’s a shared experience which creates memories that last a lifetime.</p>

<h4>Community Art Batik painting</h4>
<p>This was a unifying project involving the entire school community, including
cleaners, canteen vendors, parents, pupils, teachers, admin staff, and
school leaders. This project highlighted the equality of everyone's roles,
emphasizing that no job was less important than another.</p>

<p><strong>P4 Museum-based Learning at the National Gallery</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

</div>
<p>Aimed at making learning more authentic and for exposure to Singaporean
art, this annual learning experience for all Primary 4 pupils ensures that
every child is given at least one authentic experience of looking at a
painting in a museum in the school-based art curriculum.</p>
