Primary 1 and 2: Buzz with Cornet
---------------------------------

At a young age, pupils learn how to read notes and play a musical instrument - the cornet. Pupils who are interested in developing their talent further can consider joining the school’s Brass Band.

Primary 3: Show Choir
---------------------

As part of West View Primary’s “Engaging With Songs Programme”, pupils experience being in a “Show Choir”. They learn various performance techniques and how to dance as they sing. It is an enjoyable musical experience for pupils.
