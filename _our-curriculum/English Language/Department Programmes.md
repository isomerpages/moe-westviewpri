<p><strong>Primary 1 and 2 Speech and Drama</strong>
</p>
<p>Our Primary 1 and 2 pupils participate in the Speech and Drama Enrichment
Programme. Through the enriching experience of learning to dramatise a
story and putting together a performance for an audience, our pupils develop
proficiencies in vocal expression, and spatial awareness and stage presence.</p>
<p><strong>Lower primary pupils building their oracy skills through Speech and Drama</strong>
