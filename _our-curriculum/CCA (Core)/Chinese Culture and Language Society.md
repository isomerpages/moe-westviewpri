-----------------------------------------

![Chinese Culture and Language Society](/images/Pic%201.jpeg)

中文学会介绍 Introduction
-------------------

“中文学会”是以华语进行的课程辅助活动，学生能够通过这项活动学习华族传统文化、华语表演技能，并有更多机会展示才艺，从而对华文产生浓厚的兴趣，提升华文综合能力。

  

Chinese Culture and Language Society is a CCA conducted in Chinese. In our CCA, students learn more about Chinese culture through different performance skills and fun activities. Students have a lot opportunities to showcase their talents, so that they will become more interested in Chinese language and improve their comprehension capability.

活动内容 Content
------------

**传统文化：**文学经典、书法、剪纸、茶艺

**Traditional culture:** Literary, Calligraphy, Paper-cutting, Tea art  

  

**演艺课程：**童话剧表演、本地新谣、绕口令、讲故事、朗诵、演讲、小记者、快板

**Courses:** Fairy Tale Drama Performance, Xinyao, Tongue Twister, Storytelling, Recitation, Speech, Little Reporter, Allegro

  

**实践活动：**全国华语比赛、学校表演、 观看戏剧

**Activities:** National Chinese Language Competitions, Performances in school, Watch drama
